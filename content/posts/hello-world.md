## What is Hugo?

Hugo is one of the most popular open-source static site generators. With its amazing speed and flexibility, Hugo makes building websites fun again.

## Why Hugo?

- **Fast**: Hugo is the fastest tool of its kind
- **Flexible**: Hugo provides a robust templating system
- **Simple**: No dependencies, just one binary
